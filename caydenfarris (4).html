About

A life built on purpose

I'm Cayden Farris — Product Manager, SaaS founder, Maxwell certified coach and speaker, author, and someone who has spent his whole life finding the ceiling in every room and raising it.

I served as Assistant to the Mission President in Honduras — a top <1% leadership role — managing a 160-person organization at age 20. After returning, I financed 100% of college through full-time work, graduated with a 3.8 GPA in Accounting, broke all-time performance records at a GovTech company within 30 days, got promoted from Tier 1 support to Product Manager, and founded my own SaaS platform while doing it.

I write and speak on what I've actually lived — the pattern of entering any environment, learning faster than everyone else, and building something that lasts. That's what Under Construction is about.

The Book

Build the house before you need to look back

Most people spend years becoming who they are — and only realize too late that they built the wrong thing. Under Construction is about doing the work now, while you're young, while the foundation is still being laid.

The Mercy Seat — Where Heaven Touches Earth

The kapporet was not just a lid on a box. It was the instrument of atonement — pure gold, no human material, the one place in all the universe where God said "there I will meet with thee." Paul calls Christ the hilastērion — the mercy seat — in Romans 3:25. He doesn't say Christ brings a sacrifice to the mercy seat. He says Christ IS the mercy seat. Every approach to God — prayer, sacrament, temple — is an approach to the kapporet.

Cherem — Sacred Destruction and the Old Testament's Last Word

The Hebrew word cherem appears throughout the Old Testament and carries a dual meaning: sacred destruction and total consecration. The entire Hebrew Bible ends with cherem — Malachi 4:6 — hanging over the earth as an unresolved threat. Then Moroni quotes that same verse to Joseph Smith. The loop closes in Kirtland in 1836.

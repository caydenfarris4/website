What This Book Is About

Four foundations of principled leadership

01

The Blueprint

Before you can build anything worth keeping, you need a plan. Most people skip this step and spend years rebuilding what they should have built right the first time.

02

The Foundation

Character isn't built in moments of crisis — it's built in the ordinary decisions nobody sees. The foundation you lay today holds everything that comes after.

03

The Frame

Your values are the frame of everything you'll ever lead. The question isn't whether you have them — it's whether you built them intentionally or inherited them by accident.

04

The Roof

Leadership protects others. The person you become has to be someone other people can trust to stand under in a storm. This is what it means to finish the house.

"Most people spend decades becoming who they are, and only realize too late they built the wrong thing. This book is about doing it right while you still can."

— Under Construction, Cayden Farris
